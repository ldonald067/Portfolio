LaTanya Donaldson

Aside from loving all things Design and Technology; I am fanatic about cats, music, and the outdoors.

A native of Oakland, California I grew up being the “computer wiz” in my family. I used Google to fix various issues that came with the bargain computers my dad got me. Looking back on my childhood, technology was something that I had a knack for. I was driven to learn more and solve problems on my own.

Throughout my education I never took any graphic design or programming classes. I have a hands on learning approach. My favorite classes were metal shop, wood work, and pottery.Without realizing it, my design process was already beginning to define itself. It’s rewarding to take something I envisioned from concept to creation. Upon graduating from University of Southern California, I traveled the world because office life did not sound appealing to me. In Australia I took online coding courses such as Codecademy.

This sparked my interest in technology tremendously. I continued to push myself to learn knowing that an incredible career awaits me. After my travels, I moved to Seattle to take an intro to programming course at Epicodus. The Design section stuck out the most. Prototyping webpages in Figma (Sketch,etc) brought a great deal of unexecpected joy. I transitioned from Epicodus knowing that I want a career as an UI Designer. Now I have jumped feet-first into web design where I continue to expand my ability to code and learn. By building a solid foundation in design, I am capable of bringing my designs to life across all platforms and devices.
